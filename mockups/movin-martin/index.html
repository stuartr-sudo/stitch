Kia ora! I'm Movin' Martin!

I'm Hamilton's active travel dog and I love walking, biking, and scooting around our awesome city. Come with me and I'll teach you how to be safe on the road and have heaps of fun getting moving!

Martin's Top Tips

Stay Safe, Stay Moving!

Martin's got five important rules for every road safety superstar

⛑️

Helmet On, Right & Tight

Every time you ride your bike or scooter, make sure your helmet fits snug and is buckled up!

Be bright, be seen!
👁️

Check Before You Step

Stop at the edge, look left, look right, and listen. Cross when it's safe!

Stop, look and listen!
🏠

Sneaky Driveways

Watch out for cars backing out of driveways. They might not see you!

Stay alert, be aware!
🚶

Footpath Safety

Share the path with others. Walk, bike, and scoot safely on shared paths.

Cross like a boss!
⭐

Walk or Wheels Superstar

Walk, bike, scooter, or skate to school. Active travel keeps you healthy and helps our planet!
